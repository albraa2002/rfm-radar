# 🦅 The RFM Profit Radar | VIP Customer Retention Engine

## 📌 The Business Problem
Companies often burn massive marketing budgets on acquiring new customers (CPA), while silently losing their most valuable asset: their "Whales". The top 20% of customers typically generate 80% of the revenue. When a VIP customer slowly stops buying, the company loses massive Lifetime Value (LTV) without management even noticing until it's too late.

## 🚀 The Strategic Solution
**The RFM Profit Radar** is a predictive Business Intelligence engine designed to monitor customer lifecycles and prevent high-value churn. By implementing an RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) matrix, the system automatically segments customers and identifies anomalous behavior.

* 🟢 **Champions:** High-spending, frequent, and recent buyers. 
* 🔴 **At-Risk VIPs (Red Alert):** Customers who historically spent a lot and bought frequently, but have suddenly stopped purchasing. The system isolates them into a "Rescue List" for immediate Account Manager intervention.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack & Architecture
* **Data Engineering & RFM Scoring:** Python (Pandas)
* **Data Transfer:** JSON
* **Frontend Visualization:** Custom HTML5, CSS3, Vanilla JavaScript, Chart.js
* **UI/UX Aesthetic:** Tech Noir / Glassmorphism Command Center

## 📈 Strategic Value
Transforms customer retention from a guessing game into a targeted, data-driven operation. By flagging "At-Risk VIPs" and calculating the exact "Revenue at Risk," this dashboard provides C-level executives with the actionable insights needed to protect their bottom line and maximize Customer Lifetime Value (LTV).
